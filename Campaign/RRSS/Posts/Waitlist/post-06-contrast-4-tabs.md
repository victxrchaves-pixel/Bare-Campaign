**Type:** Single image — contrast, problem as backdrop

## Visual

**Background:** #FAFAFA  
**Layout:** Two-part — statement top, answer bottom, separated by line

```
YOU HAVE 4 TABS
OPEN WHILE TRYING
TO CREATE.


────────────────────


BARE IS ONE.

bare · waitlist in bio
```

**Typography:**
- "YOU HAVE 4 TABS / OPEN WHILE TRYING / TO CREATE." → Switzer Regular, ~72pt, #101010, left-aligned, 8% margin, top half of frame
- Separator line → 1px solid #101010, full width minus margins, vertical center
- "BARE IS ONE." → Switzer SemiBold, ~100pt, #101010, left-aligned, bottom half
- "bare · waitlist in bio" → Geist Light, ~18pt, #101010, letter-spacing 0.25em, bottom-left

**Notes for designer:**
- The weight contrast between the problem (Regular) and the answer (SemiBold) is intentional
- "BARE IS ONE." should feel heavier — it's the resolution
- Keep the separator centered and clean
